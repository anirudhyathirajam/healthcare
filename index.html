Bringing The Furture of HealthCare

India's healthcare system faces challenges such as overcrowded hospitals, a shortage of doctors,and limited access to medical facilities in rural areas. The aim is to develop an AI-powered Smart Healthcare Assistant that tackles these issues by enabling early disease detection, personalized diagnostics, and remote patient monitoring.

About

Chatbot – A friendly AI that chats with you, answers questions, and helps with tasks.
Disease Prediction System – An AI-powered tool that looks at symptoms and gives possible health insights.
AI Assistant – A smart helper that automates tasks, finds information, and keeps you productive.
Our goal is simple: to bring AI into everyday life in a way that actually helps people. Stay tuned for more awesome stuff!
